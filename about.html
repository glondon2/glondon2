Jared's story

began by choosing to study his passion, Music, with a minor in Management at Northeastern University in the fall of 2008. In college, Jared was a full time manager of many hip-hop artists in a professional Co-Op program, but spent most of his time working with principle artist Jitta on the Track. Jared created and managed his own Music label, Rec This City.

In the winter of 2012, at merely 22 years old, Jared helped Jitta on the Track land a spot on Drake’s Club Paradise Tour, touring together with the likes of Drake, Meek Mill, 2 Chainz, Lil Wayne and more.

Jared's prior experiences in Artist Management then led him to get an opportunity to be a direct assistant to popular recording artist Lil Wayne. Jared stayed at Young Money for roughly a year.

Eager to gain experience in a more business friendly setting, Jared was hired as a logistics manager at startup Uncharted Play, where he honed in on his strategic thinking and entrepreneurial background for almost four years to help the company be more efficient with their products and resources, and generate invaluable R&D.

Jared has moved on and now consults in a variety of industries, maintaining relationships with Artists, Managers, Consultants, and Executives around the country.
